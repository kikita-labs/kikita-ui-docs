  • The host sets role="progressbar", aria-valuemin="0", and aria-valuemax="100".
  • Determinate progress additionally sets aria-valuenow from value.
  • Indeterminate progress (value omitted or null) does not set aria-valuenow, matching native indeterminate semantics.
  • Always provide an accessible name with aria-label or aria-labelledby. Progress does not infer one from projected content.
